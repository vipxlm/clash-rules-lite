极简主义 Minimalism

核心在于“少即是多”。通过大量留白、简洁的元素、功能至上的设计、有限的调色板和清晰的排版，实现极致的纯粹与高效。

Core: whitespace, simplicity, function-first, limited color palette, clean typography, less is more.
扁平化设计 Flat Design

摒弃装饰性元素如阴影和渐变，采用纯色块、简约图标和清晰的排版，构建干净利落的二维界面，注重可用性。

Focus: no shadows, no gradients, solid colors, simple icons, clean typography, 2D interface.
质感设计 Material Design

谷歌倡导，以物理世界的隐喻为基础，通过卡片式布局、Z轴深度感、有意义的动画、响应式交互和大胆的图形元素，创造直观体验。

Google's style: material metaphor, card-based layout, z-depth, meaningful animation, responsive interaction, bold graphics.
苹果设计风格 Apple Design

强调清晰、直观和一致性。通过适度的模糊与阴影营造深度感，运用优雅的过渡动画，始终以内容为中心进行设计。

Principles: clarity, intuitive, consistency, depth (subtle blur & shadow), elegant transitions, content-centric.


微软流畅设计 Fluent Design

围绕光线、深度、动效、材质和缩放五大核心元素构建。追求跨设备的一致性和沉浸式的用户体验。

Elements: light, depth, motion, material, scale, ensuring cross-device consistency.
玻璃拟态 Glassmorphism

通过背景模糊、半透明材质、细腻的边框和悬浮感，创造出富有层次感和现代感的界面，如同磨砂玻璃一般。

Features: background blur, translucency, subtle borders, floating feel, creating a layered, modern look.

🧊
新拟态 Neumorphism

利用柔和的内外阴影，在单色背景上模拟出元素被挤压或凸起的效果，营造简约界面下的触感错觉。

Style: soft shadows, extruded/pressed effect on a monochromatic background, minimalist UI with a tactile illusion.
粗野主义 Brutalism

追求原始的HTML感，通过鲜明对比、单调字体、无修饰的元素，展现大胆、实验性甚至反主流的设计态度。

Characterized by: raw HTML feel, stark contrast, monospaced fonts, unadorned, bold, experimental, anti-establishment.

RAW
便当网格 Bento UI

采用模块化布局，将信息组织成独立的信息块。通过不对称网格和清晰的视觉层级，实现内容的高效展示。

Layout: modular layout with information blocks, often an asymmetrical grid, clear visual hierarchy for content organization and efficient display.
现代风格 Modern

以简洁线条、大图背景、清晰排版为特点，强调响应式设计、用户友好性和功能驱动，是当前主流的网站设计趋势。

Traits: clean lines, large hero images, clear typography, responsive, user-friendly, and function-driven.
复古/怀旧风格 Retro/Vintage

借鉴旧时代的设计元素，如复古字体、纹理背景、褪色效果，并采用特定历史时期（如50年代、80年代、Y2K）的配色方案。

Incorporates: old-era elements, retro fonts, textured backgrounds, faded effects, and period-specific color palettes (e.g., 50s, 80s, Y2K).
手绘/插画风格 Illustrated

通过个性化的创意插图、手写字体和有机的形状，营造亲和力，赋予网站独特的故事感和艺术气息。

Emphasizes: personalization, creative illustrations, handwritten fonts, organic shapes, approachability, and storytelling.
暗黑模式 Dark Mode

采用深色背景和高对比度文本，有助于节省能源（OLED屏幕），减轻眼部疲劳，并赋予界面现代感和优雅气质。

Uses: dark background, high-contrast text, aids energy saving (OLED), reduced eye strain, offering a modern and elegant feel.

🌙
瑞士风格 Swiss Style

注重网格系统、无衬线字体和不对称布局。追求客观清晰的信息层级和功能性的美学表达。

Focuses on: grid system, sans-serif typography, asymmetrical layout, objective clarity, information hierarchy, and functional aesthetics.
大标题排版 Hero Typography

运用巨型字体制造视觉冲击，直接传递核心信息。设计以排版为主导，辅以简约元素和个性化字体选择。

Utilizes: oversized fonts for visual impact and direct messaging, typography-led design with minimalist support and custom fonts.
单页/视差滚动 Single Page

通过线性叙事和滚动交互，结合动态效果，营造强烈的故事感。通常采用移动优先策略和简洁的导航设计。

Features: linear narrative, scroll-based interaction, dynamic effects, strong storytelling, often mobile-first with simple navigation.
孟菲斯设计 Memphis

以大胆的几何图形、明亮的撞色、波点与条纹图案为特征，充满玩味性，是80年代复古风格的代表，敢于打破常规。

Known for: bold geometrics, bright color clashes, polka dots/stripes, playfulness, 80s retro, and rule-breaking.

~
3D 元素/插画 3D Elements

通过立体感、动态效果和交互式3D技术，提供沉浸式体验，常用于产品展示，富有科技感。

Offers: dimensionality, dynamic effects, interactive 3D for an immersive experience, great for product showcase, with a tech feel.
赛博朋克 Cyberpunk

运用霓虹灯效、暗色调、未来科技感、故障艺术和代码元素，描绘反乌托邦的数字世界。

Visuals: neon effects, dark tones, futuristic tech, glitch art, code elements, creating a dystopian vibe.
不对称布局 Asymmetry

通过动态平衡和视觉引导，打破常规布局，赋予设计现代感和创意自由，同时营造层次感。

Achieves: dynamic balance, visual guidance, breaking conventions for a modern feel with creative freedom and a sense of depth.
照片驱动设计 Photography

以高质量图片和全屏背景为主导，通过视觉叙事建立情感连接，文字元素简约，图片优先。

Led by: high-quality imagery, full-screen backgrounds, visual storytelling for emotional connection, with minimal text, image-first.
奢侈/高端风格 Luxury

采用精致的排版、优雅的配色、高质量的素材和充足的留白，注重细节考究，营造尊贵感。

Defined by: refined typography, elegant color palettes, premium assets, ample whitespace, meticulous details, evoking a sense of prestige.
抽象艺术风格 Abstract

运用非具象的图形、富有情感的色彩和动态的构图，进行概念表达，营造独特的艺术氛围和视觉效果。

Uses: non-representational graphics, color emotion, dynamic composition for conceptual expression, an artistic vibe, and unique visuals.
拼贴风格 Collage

通过多元素的组合、纹理的叠加，营造复古感和手作感。常采用不规则排版，使视觉效果丰富多样。

Builds with: multi-element composition, texture overlay, a vintage feel or handmade touch, irregular layout for a visually rich result.

AB
可持续/环保主题 Eco-friendly

采用自然色系、有机纹理和环保图标，传递清晰的环保信息和使命感，是典型的绿色设计。

Features: natural color palettes, organic textures, eco-icons, clear messaging, mission-driven, embodying green design.
